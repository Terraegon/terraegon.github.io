How to set up your profile

  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Add any extra information to your profile page by typing it into the chat box underneath your icon.
  3. Go to this link and clone the spreadsheet. Fill the spreadsheet out, keeping in mind that you have 100 XP (to begin with) to assign to different attributes.
  4. Get the shareable link to your spreadsheet and copy and paste it into your profile page's chat box. You're done!
