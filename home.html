Hello, fellow writers, and welcome to Terraegon! To put it simply, Terraegon is a private RPG (or role-playing game) website. The name is based on the herb, tarragon (and the name of a Prodigy 2nd evolution dragon), and the Latin stems, terra and gon, which mean earth and shape. Unsurprisingly, Terra is also the name of delicious vegetable chips. Anyway, I'm getting sidetracked. If you mash those stems together, you get Terraegon or earth-shape. The shape of the earth is not exactly a perfect sphere, but rather has slight blemishes and bumps. Kind of like a portal to a mystical realm, right? And who can resist the dragon, earth, shape, and food combination?

Check out the Guidelines tab for more information about roleplay and its rules!
